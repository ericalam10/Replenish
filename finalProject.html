Who We Are

We are three rising seniors who live in the San Francisco Bay Area. We are all part of the Girls Who Summer Immersion program at Amazon Web Services in Palo Alto. Our goal is to spread environemental awareness through creating this website. Here, anyone can read articles about the effects of climate change and find out how they can help keep our planet clean.
